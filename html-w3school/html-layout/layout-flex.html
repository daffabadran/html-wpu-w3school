Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan, and the most populous city in the world. It is known for its modern architecture, bustling streets, and rich cultural heritage.

Osaka

Osaka is Japan's third largest city, famous for its modern skyline, vibrant nightlife, and delicious street food such as takoyaki and okonomiyaki. It is also known for its friendly people and historical landmarks like Osaka Castle.

Kyoto

Kyoto is renowned for its classical Buddhist temples, beautiful gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. It was the capital of Japan for over a millennium.
